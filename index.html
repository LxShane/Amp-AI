Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Try any plan free for 14 days. No credit card required. If you love it (we think you will), continue with your chosen plan. If not, cancel anytime—no hard feelings.

Do I need to change my phone number?

Nope! Keep your existing number. We set up call forwarding from your phone to our AI. Your customers call the same number they always have.

What happens during emergencies?

Our AI is trained to detect emergencies (gas leaks, major flooding, electrical hazards). It immediately escalates to you via phone call with full context.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are live within 24-48 hours. We handle all the technical setup—you just answer a few questions about your services and availability.

Can I still answer calls myself?

Absolutely! With conditional forwarding, your phone rings first. If you don't answer after 3 rings, the AI takes over. You're always in control.
